Managing Diabetes
Our expert clinicians believe effective control of Type 2 Diabetes is grounded in Measurement-Based Care or “MBC.” The foundational principle of MBC is reliance on real-time and frequent monitoring of blood glucose levels to optimize medical decision making and, thus, health outcomes. To do this, StatusWell deploys active monthly remote patient monitoring by obtaining blood glucose levels from continuous glucose monitoring “CGM” devices and then aggregating the data from our patients through our proprietary Artificial Intelligence (AI) enabled risk tool.
This sophisticated methodology enables our expert clinical team to maintain a vigilant and proactive stance in managing diabetes. With access to precise data, our clinicians can closely monitor each patient's progress, evaluate their condition, and intervene when necessary by adjusting medication regimens. Our team of expert clinicians can then offer tailored recommendations for dietary modifications and lifestyle adjustments to enhance your overall health outcomes.
