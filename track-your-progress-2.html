At StatusWell, we firmly believe that the key to achieving enhanced weight loss and effective diabetic care lies in the practice of Measurement-Based Care, or "MBC." Our approach revolves around providing active and real-time support to members and patients as they navigate their path to improved outcomes. This is achieved through a comprehensive strategy that involves frequent digital and virtual communication with valuable feedback, the utilization of continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) for risk analysis feedback, interactive sessions with our dedicated Wellness Coaches, and continuous engagement with our accomplished Doctors, Nurse Practitioners, Pharmacists, and Wellness Coaches. Our primary objective is to become a trusted daily partner for patients, fostering patient engagement and education while ensuring timely medication adjustments.
