StatusWell
Reduce Risk
StatusWell is committed to its core mission of reducing overall risk factors for members and patients who are pre-diabetic or Type 2 diabetic. Extensive research indicates that risk factors such as obesity, reduced physical activity, and a family history of diabetes significantly contribute to an elevated susceptibility to Type 2 Diabetes.
The health consequences associated with pre-diabetes and diabetes are substantive and, if left unaddressed, can lead to more severe medical conditions, including but not limited to heart disease, kidney failure, compromised circulatory health, and even vision impairment.
At StatusWell, our highly skilled clinical team maintains ongoing communication with our patients by leveraging digital tools and virtual processes. This dynamic interaction is designed to achieve the following objectives:
Enhance dietary and nutritional choices
Promote and sustain appropriate levels of physical activity

Prescribe and oversee the management of suitable prescription medications
