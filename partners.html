Primary Care Providers
Hospitals & Health Systems
StatusWell collaborates with primary care providers in an effort to identify patients with metabolic health needs. We offer coordinated care that removes the guesswork in recommending appropriate diabetic and weight care interventions.

Research reports that diabetes increases the risk for 30-day hospital readmission by at least 17% and up to 2.5-fold. We offer coordinated care with primary goals in reducing hospital readmission risk and improving patient outcomes.

Long-term Care Communities
StatusWell offers programs that focus on improving patient outcomes and reducing costly hospital admissions. Our approach streamlines an effective care delivery model that works with your ecosystem and ensures member satisfaction.
Diabetes is more common in older adults, has a high prevalence in long-term care communities and is associated with significant disease burden and higher cost. We partner with assisted living facilities, skilled nursing facilities and other long-term care communities to offer patients accessible care in an effort to reduce cost and improve outcomes.
