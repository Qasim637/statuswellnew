Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM)
CGM involves a small device that tracks your glucose levels (blood sugar) in real-time. A CGM sensor is applied generally to the back of your upper arm where the sensor can constantly read your glucose level for 14 days. The CGM sensor sends this information directly to the StatusWell app where you can view your glucose trends overtime. Your provider will use this information to adjust and personalize your treatment plan.
