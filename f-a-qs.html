StatusWell is a healthcare platform that offers personalized diabetes and weight care to individuals. Our providers utilize continuous glucose monitoring technology paired with an AI-enabled app to customize a treatment plan that fits your individual healthcare goals and needs.
⦁ All of our programs include access to our StatusWell app which allows you to meet with your provider via telehealth to customize your treatment plan and goals.
⦁ Our providers initially prescribe continuous glucose monitors (CGM) to gain better insight on your current health status and your personal needs.
⦁ Your provider may prescribe necessary prescription medications to help you achieve your goals. You’ll also get access to our partnering pharmacy medication services.
⦁ We also offer personalized wellness and diabetic education coaching, live diet monitoring advice and reinforcement via Chat and optional customized diet plans.
